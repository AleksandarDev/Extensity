Extensity by sergiokas

Quickly enable/disable Google Chrome extensions

Tired of having too much extensions in your toolbar? Try Extensity, a quick access extension for lightning fast enabling and disabling all your extensions for Google Chrome. Just enable the extension when you want to use it, and disable when you want to get ride ot it for a little while. You can also launch Chrome Apps right from the list. * Keep your browser fast - disable extensions that you won't use right away. * Keep your toolbar clean * Ideal companion for extensions collectors. Extensity is free software. Full source code at GitHub https://github.com/sergiokas/Extensity
